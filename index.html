App Description

Battery Guardian is a free, simple, and lightweight app designed to help you monitor battery health, prevent overheating, and extend battery lifespan. Get timely alerts when your battery level or temperature goes beyond configurable limits, track battery consumption history, and see which apps are draining your battery.
